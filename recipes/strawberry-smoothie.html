Strawberry Smoothie

Description

An easy to make Healthy Strawberry Smoothie recipe, that is creamy and sweet to start the day with the right foot!

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen strawberries
  • Frozen banana
  • Milk of your choice
  • Yougurt (optional)
  • You may swap strawberries with another fruit as you see fit.

Steps

  1. Put all the Ingredients in a blender in the order below:
  2. Add the milk.
  3. Add the Yogurt, if using.
  4. Add the fruit.
  5. Add the Ice, if using.
  6. Blend and serve!
